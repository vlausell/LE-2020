LEGAL ENGLISH

 This LEGAL ENGLISH course is designed to introduce you to the legal English which you will need to study abroad or to work as a lawyer in an international legal context. Since the vast majority of lawyers in the world deal with commercial law, apart from introducing the terminology for criminal and civil law, the course focuses on the use of English for this purpose. Within the field of business law, and in view of the time constraints of a sixty-hour course, the course focuses on the areas of company law and contracts.
There are links to readings, listenings , interactive exercises or other activitiesfor each unit to do or prepare at home. You will be expected to come to class prepared with the homework assignments done so that you can participate in the day's activities.
